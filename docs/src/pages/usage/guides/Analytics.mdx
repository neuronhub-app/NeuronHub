### Jobs Alert
- `job_alert__open__email` - emails contain tracked urls as `https://{url}/{job.slug}?email=true`
- `job_alert__subscribe__open`
    - `facets: list[str]`
- `job_alert__subscribe__click__submit`
    - `is_success: boolean`
    - `facets: list[str]`
- `job_alert__subscribe__click__email`
    - `facets: list[str]`
- `job_alert__subscribe__click__close`
    - `facets: list[str]`
    - `button: Escape | CloseButton | OuterClick`
- `job_alert__subscribe__click__content`
    - `facets: list[str]`

### Job List
- `job__list__click`
- `job__list__sort`
    - `value: "posted_at" | "closes_at"`
- `job__list__facet_reset`
    - `value: str`
- `job__list__facet_all`
    - `value: list[str]`
- `job__list__facet`
    - `attribute: str`
    - `value: str`

### Job Card
- `job__card__click__content`
    - `id: {job.id}`
- `job__card__click__url`
    - `id: {job.id}`
    - `value: {org.domain}`
- `job__card__click__org`
    - `value: {domain}`
    - `id: {job.id}`
