Sent daily at `8am` in the user's timezone.

### User Story

1. <Term id="backend" /> reviews every hour the new Jobs and which active <PageLink id="JobAlert"/>s
   haven't been notified about the newly created `Jobs`.
    - <Text color="fg.muted">`hourly` is 24 times per day at `XX:00` minutes.</Text>
2. <Term id="backend" /> selects a <PageLink id="JobAlert"/> and sends the template to its `.email`.
    1. <Term id="backend" /> creates <PageLink id="JobAlertLog"/>.
    2. <Term id="backend" /> sends the Task to send email to its background worker.
        - The admin panel allows you to view the <PageLink id="TaskResult"/>s success/failure.
        - The <PageLink id="Sentry" hash="scheduled-tasks-monitor">Sentry's Scheduled Tasks Monitor</PageLink>
          lets you view all scheduled successful/failed executions.


Worst-case delay is 23h.
